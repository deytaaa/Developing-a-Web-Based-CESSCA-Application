# CESSCA System User Guide

## How to Register and Log In

### Registering for a CESSCA Account
1. Go to the CESSCA Portal website.
2. Click the **Register** link below the login form or in the navigation bar.
3. Fill out the registration form:
	- **First Name, Middle Name, Last Name**: Enter your full legal name.
	- **Role**: Select your role (Student, Organization Officer, or Alumni).
	- **Student ID**: (If applicable) Enter your student ID number.
	- **Course/Program**: Select your course or program from the dropdown.
	- **Email Address**: Enter your valid PTC email address (e.g., your.email@ptc.edu.ph).
	- **Password**: Create a password (minimum 6 characters).
	- **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password to confirm.
4. Double-check all information for accuracy.
5. Click **Create Account**.
6. You will see a confirmation message: “Registration Successful! Your account has been created and is pending approval.”
7. Wait for an administrator to approve your account. You will receive an email or notification once approved.

### Logging In to CESSCA
1. Go to the CESSCA Portal website.
2. Enter your registered email address and password in the login form.
3. Click **Sign In**.
4. If your credentials are correct and your account is approved, you will be redirected to your dashboard.
5. If you see an error (e.g., “Login failed. Please try again.”), check your email and password, or wait for account approval.


**Note:** Only authorized PTC students, organization officer, and alumni can register. Each user must use their real information for verification and approval.

## Student Sidebar Walkthrough
1. **Dashboard**
	- Welcome banner with your name and role.
	- Stats cards (organizations joined, achievements, etc.).
	- Announcements (school/org, urgent, recent).
	- Quick access buttons (Profile, Organizations, Achievements).
2. **Organizations**
	- List of all active student organizations (with logo, acronym, type).
	- Click to view details: info, officers, members, gallery.
	- If not a member: request to join (button).
	- If pending: see request status.
	- If member: see org events, announcements, and gallery.
3. **Discipline**
	- View your own discipline/consultation records (read-only).
	- Each record: infraction, date, status, remarks.
	- Cannot add or edit records.
4. **Arts, Culture & Sports**
	- View all school sports, cultural, and arts events/activities.
	- Browse event details, results, and gallery.
	- Gallery: view photos by category/year.
5. **Achievements**
	- View school-wide and personal achievements (cards with title, desc, date, images).
6. **About PTC**
	- Read about PTC: mission, vision, core values, admin info.
7. **Profile**
	- View and edit your profile (name, contact, address, etc.).
	- Upload/change profile picture.
8. **Logout**
	- Securely log out.

## CESSCA Staff Sidebar Walkthrough
1. **Dashboard**
	- Welcome banner, staff stats, announcements.
	- Quick access to user/org management.
2. **Organizations**
	- Manage all organizations (approve/reject members, edit, delete, view details).
	- Access all orgs, not just your own.
3. **Activities**
	- Review and approve all org activity proposals.
	- View, approve, reject, or comment on activities.
4. **Discipline**
	- View and manage discipline/consultation records for all users.
	- Assign, update, or resolve cases.
5. **Alumni**
	- View and edit alumni profile, add achievement, and add post-graduate.
6. **Arts, Culture & Sports**
	- View, add, edit and delete, all school sports, cultural, and arts events/activities.
	- Upload/manage and delete gallery images (staff only).
7. **Achievements**
	- Manage and review all achievements (school/org/alumni).
8. **About PTC**
	- View About PTC: mission, vision, core values, admin info.
	- CESSCA staff can edit About PTC content and upload images.
9. **Administration**
	 - Access Administration panel (limited):
		 - Manage Organizations (view, edit, approve/reject)
		 - Manage Announcements (create, edit, delete)
	 - Cannot access User Management or Activity Logs (admin only)
10. **Profile**
	- View and edit your staff profile.
11. **Logout**
	- Securely log out.
