# CESSCA System User Guide

This guide explains how each user type (Student, Officer, Alumni, CESSCA Staff, Admin) interacts with the CESSCA web application, including login, dashboard, navigation, and the functionality of each page and button.

---

## 1. Student

### Login
- Go to the login page.
- Enter your email and password.
- Click **Login**.

### After Login: Dashboard
- **Banner**: Welcome banner at the top.
- **Stats Cards**: Quick stats (e.g., total organizations, events, etc.).
- **Announcements**: Urgent and recent announcements are shown.
- **Quick Access**: Shortcuts to key pages (Profile, Organizations, Achievements, etc.).

#### Dashboard Buttons & Cards
- **Profile**: View and edit your personal information.
- **Organizations**: Browse and join organizations. See pending requests.
- **Achievements**: View school and personal achievements.
- **Activities/Events**: See upcoming and past events.
- **Logout**: Log out of the system.

#### Other Pages
- **About**: Learn about CESSCA and the school.
- **Gallery**: View photos from events and organizations.
- **Service Requests**: Submit requests for help or support.

---

## 2. Officer

### Login
- Go to the login page.
- Enter your officer credentials.
- Click **Login**.

### After Login: Dashboard
- **Banner**: Welcome banner at the top.
- **Stats Cards**: Officer-specific stats (e.g., organization members, events managed).
- **Announcements**: Urgent and recent announcements.
- **Quick Access**: Shortcuts to manage organizations, view logs, etc.

#### Dashboard Buttons & Cards
- **Profile**: View/edit your info.
- **Organizations**: Manage your organization, approve/reject membership requests.
- **Achievements**: Add/view achievements for your organization.
- **Activities/Events**: Create and manage events.
- **Logout**: Log out.

#### Other Pages
- **About, Gallery, Service Requests**: Same as student, with additional management options where applicable.

---

## 3. Alumni

### Login
- Go to the login page.
- Enter your alumni credentials.
- Click **Login**.

### After Login: Dashboard
- **Banner**: Welcome banner at the top.
- **Stats Cards**: Alumni stats (e.g., events attended, organizations joined).
- **Quick Access**: Shortcuts to profile, achievements, etc.

#### Dashboard Buttons & Cards
- **Profile**: View/edit info.
- **Achievements**: View school and personal achievements.
- **Organizations**: View organizations (joining may be restricted).
- **Logout**: Log out.

#### Other Pages
- **About, Gallery, Service Requests**: Same as student.

---

## 4. CESSCA Staff

### Login
- Go to the login page.
- Enter your staff credentials.
- Click **Login**.

### After Login: Admin Panel
- **Tabs**: Users, Organizations, Announcements, Logs.
- **Badge**: Shows "CESSCA Staff Access".

#### Tabs Explained
- **Users**: View/search users, approve/reject pending users.
- **Organizations**: Manage organizations, approve/reject membership requests.
- **Announcements**: View/create/delete announcements.
- **Logs**: View activity logs.

#### Buttons & Actions
- **Approve/Reject**: For users and organization members.
- **Create**: Add new users, organizations, announcements.
- **Delete**: Remove users, organizations, announcements.
- **Edit**: Update organization details.
- **Logout**: Log out.

---

## 5. Admin

### Login
- Go to the login page.
- Enter your admin credentials.
- Click **Login**.

### After Login: Admin Panel
- **Tabs**: Users, Organizations, Announcements, Logs (same as CESSCA Staff, but with full access).
- **Badge**: Shows "Admin Access".

#### Tabs Explained
- **Users**: Full user management (create, edit, delete, approve, search).
- **Organizations**: Full organization management.
- **Announcements**: Full announcement management.
- **Logs**: Full access to activity logs.

#### Buttons & Actions
- **Create/Edit/Delete**: Users, organizations, announcements.
- **Approve/Reject**: Users and organization members.
- **View Logs**: See all system activity.
- **Logout**: Log out.

---

## General Navigation
- Use the sidebar or top navigation to switch between pages.
- Use the **Logout** button to securely exit your account.

---

For more details, refer to the in-app tooltips and help sections, or contact CESSCA support.
